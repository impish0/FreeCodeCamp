Hogates Famous Blueberry Pie Recipe

Turns out the best blueberry pie you can eat, requires a 4-hour flight to Anchorage Alaska and a 6 hour dirve from Anchorage to Denali. Only after that treck will you find the berries that equally satisfy the Black Bears and the humans.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Denali Blueberries
  • 1 TBSP of butter
  • 2 Cups of Wheat Flower
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Mash all the blueberries together
  2. Boil them with some water to soften
  3. Start oven at 350 degrees
  4. Back for an hour and 45 minutes
